About Us

Radiant Beauty & Aesthetics Centre

Radiant Skin is a medical spa in Sabah, Malaysia, focused on addressing common skin concerns like acne, scars, and wrinkles. Our advanced facials are designed to combat signs of aging and promote overall skin rejuvenation.

We offer a wide range of beauty services including lash extensions, eyebrow shaping, facial treatments, and more. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to helping you achieve radiant, healthy skin.
