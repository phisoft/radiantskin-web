About Us

“ Our Story

Healthy Skin.
Genuine Confidence.

Radiant Skin was founded in 2022 with a simple vision—to help people achieve healthier, naturally radiant skin through professional skin care, personalised medi-facial programmes and doctor-supervised aesthetic services.

Founded by Dr. Maung Khaing Lynn (Dr. Khai) and his wife Clorisa Jossin, Radiant Skin brings together medical expertise, nursing care and a genuine passion for skin health. Together, they believe that confidence begins with healthy skin, and that every individual deserves personalised care in a safe, welcoming and professional environment.

DK
Dr. Maung Khaing Lynn (Dr. Khai)
Licensed Medical Doctor & Co-Founder

Dr. Khai is a licensed medical doctor with qualifications in primary care dermatology and aesthetic medicine. He is certified with the Letter of Credentialing and Privileging (LCP) in Aesthetic Medical Practice issued by the Ministry of Health Malaysia, reflecting his commitment to practising aesthetic medicine according to recognised professional standards. He is also the Chief Executive Officer of Permai Polyclinics Group, the largest primary healthcare network in Sabah.

CJ
Clorisa Jossin
Registered Nurse & Co-Founder

Clorisa Jossin holds a Nursing Degree from Lincoln University College and is a Registered Nurse with the Malaysian Nursing Board. Her nursing background and compassionate, patient-centred approach help create an environment where every client feels welcomed, cared for and supported throughout their skin journey.

At Radiant Skin, we understand that every skin is unique. Rather than offering a one-size-fits-all approach, we begin with a personalised skin assessment to better understand each client's skin condition, concerns and goals. Based on this assessment, we recommend suitable medi-facial programmes, professional skincare products and aesthetic services tailored to support healthier-looking, naturally radiant skin.

Whether you wish to improve the appearance of acne-prone skin, pigmentation, uneven skin tone, dullness, dehydration or the visible signs of ageing, our team is committed to providing personalised skin solutions designed around your individual needs, lifestyle and skin goals.

"Our beauty consultants undergo continuous professional training under Dr. Khai's supervision. Every medi-facial programme follows protocols developed under his medical guidance, ensuring a consistent standard of care."
